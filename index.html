Empower 

ClimateCorps believes that sustainable workforce perpetuates a sustainable planet. Climate conservation is a complex issue, and mitigating this crisis demands all-hands on deck.

We empower current and future generations of humanity to make an impact and create a more sustainable world.

We believe in the power of human resources.

Preserve 

The industrial revolution and increasing globalization have had dire ramifications for our shared planet.

Many of today's companies are working alongside engineers, scientists, and researchers to combat climate change and reverse this crisis.

ClimateCorps alumni go on to serve some of the largest and most sustainable organizations in the world.

Protect 

Protecting our planet for future generations and their posterity is everyone's responsiblity.

So much damage has already been done, but ClimateCorps believes that the same industrial and technological advances that got us into this mess can help us transcend this crisis.

It's time to roll up our sleeves, grab a shovel or a or a test tube, and get to work!

OUR MISSION.

      Preserving the planet is everyone's responsibility. Our mission is to bring exceptional people together from all walks of life to ensure a more sustainable future for subsequent generations.

HOW WE'LL GET THERE.

      We empower current and future generations of humanity by providing career placement and training oppportuinities focusing on creating a more sustainable future for everyone.






OUR FOCUS.

      Climate conservation is a complex issue. At ClimateCorps, we aim for the highest point of contribution by focusing efforts on five key areas:

Zero Waste
Clean Water
Critical Education
Renewable Energy
Community Organizing
